LETTER FROM NORMAN LEYS TO LEONARD WOOLF
28/09/1925

				28.9.25
Dear Woolf,

		By my calculation — which I admit may be all wrong — you(?) ought to have a balance due me. If so can you send me £20 by Oct. 1st?

		Sales this month are so encouraging as to make me very hopeful of a cheap edition next Jan(?). I hope to see you about it next month when in London. I wonder

—p.2—

where most of these last sold 40 copies(?) have gone to. To America I hope.

		Yours
				Norman Leys

I suppose the thing to do would be to approach U.S.A. Lab. ??? (and the organisations?) before writing a cheap ed.
